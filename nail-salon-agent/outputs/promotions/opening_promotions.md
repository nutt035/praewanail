# Grand Opening Promotion Ideas - Antonette Nail

This document outlines the proposed opening promotions based on the `promotion_planner.md` framework.

---

## 🌟 Option 1: "Welcome Glow"
*Focus: Attracting a high volume of new customers.*

- **Promotion Name:** Welcome Glow ✨
- **Campaign Concept:** Providing a special welcome discount to first-time customers to experience premium services at an accessible price.
- **Discount Strategy:** Solid color gel nails for a special price (e.g., 199.- or 259.-) or a 20% discount on the first service.
- **Target Audience:** Students and young women who follow nail trends.
- **Expected Result:** Rapid growth of new customer base and an increase in social media reviews.

---

## 🌟 Option 2: "BFF Beauty Date"
*Focus: Word-of-mouth and group bookings.*

- **Promotion Name:** BFF Beauty Date 💖👯‍♀️
- **Campaign Concept:** "Double the beauty, double the luxury." Encouraging customers to bring a friend to create a fun, social atmosphere in the shop.
- **Discount Strategy:** 10-15% discount for both when coming in pairs, or "Second person 50% off" for gel nail services.
- **Target Audience:** Best friends and office colleagues.
- **Expected Result:** Increase in double-slot bookings and brand awareness through organic referrals.

---

## 🌟 Option 3: "Luxury Upgrade"
*Focus: Increasing Average Order Value (AOV) and showcasing full service.*

- **Promotion Name:** Luxury Upgrade Set 💅✨
- **Campaign Concept:** Bundling popular services into a single "Full Treatment" package to provide a comprehensive luxury experience.
- **Discount Strategy:** Package Bundle: Gel Polish + Removal + Nail Overlay/Reinforcement at a flat discounted rate.
- **Target Audience:** Customers seeking comprehensive care or those needing extra durability.
- **Expected Result:** Higher revenue per customer and demonstration of the salon's professional range of services.
